Spicy feta & pepper dip

delicious dip to go with anything you wants

Ingredients

  • 240 g feta cheese
  • 120 g jarred red peppers (drained weight)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Method

  1. In a food processor combine the feta, red peppers and oil.
  2. Season with black pepper and blend until smooth.
  3. Transfer to a bowl, then serve
